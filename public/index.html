PETRO NGOVOLIA EDAVO

January 6, 1940 ~~ December 23, 2017

Psalm 23:4 - "Even though I walk through the darkest valley, I will fear no evil, for you are with me; your rod and your staff, they comfort me."

Life and times

    Birth
  • January 6, 1940 - Born at Ikumba village, Maragoli Central Location, Vihiga County. Was 4th born of Mzee Hezron Edavo & Mama Trufena Ambunya. He hails from Vasaniaga clan and is uncled with the Vayongaa clan.
    • School
  • 1953 - Started schooling at Ikumba primary school where he sat for and passed the Common Entrance Examination (CEE) in 1956 and Joined Makupa Primary in Mombasa from 1957 to 1958
    • Circumcision
  • 1952 - was circumcised under the age set "Serula"
    • Work
  • 1958 - Started work with Mombasa Municipal Council as a Librarian, was promoted to Social Services Supervisor and retired in 1974 due to ill health.
  • 1978- 1987 He worked at House Mc George as Senior Clerical Officer.
    • 1988 - Joined High Freighters Kenya Limited as Senior Clerical Officer
  • 1992 - Retired to Ikumba village and ventured into farming becoming an astute farmer.
    • Marriage
  • 1967- Got married to Esther Kagai and was blessed with 7 children being 3 boys and 4 girls. All his children are still alive. He is also survived by 28 grand children and 2 great grand children.
    • Christian Life
  • He was baptised as a child by Festo Lisamadi
  • He learned the first faith (book 1) in 2011 and was baptized by Superintendent Zebedi Musudia. He learned the second faith (book 2) in 2012 by General Superintendent Krispo Amadade
    • Sickness
  • Fell ill in 1974 and was taken to Mombasa Hospital where he was diagnosed with Peptic Ulcers
  • He was taken to Oasis Doctors plaza in Kisumu on 28th April 2016 where he was diagnosed with Chronic Bilateral Lung Disease and Peptic Ulcers.
  • In June 2017 he was admitted to Mukumu Mission Hospital for 7 days and was discharged.
  • He was to later visit the following hospitals in search of medication and better health;
      • 1) Itando Mission Hospital
      2) Mbale Specialist Clinic – Cherry Home
      3) Vihiga County Referral Hospital - NHIF Clinic where he received attention until his death on Dec 23rd 2017 at 3pm.
        May his soul rest in eternal peace.
